About TripWell

Our Story

TripWell was founded by Adam Cole, who needed help planning trips with his teenage daughter as they ventured around Europe. Adam recognized that 'be spontaneous' planning is not ideal when traveling across the globe and wanting to do everything possible.

Using cutting-edge AI technology, we analyze millions of data points to create personalized itineraries that match your unique travel style, interests, and preferences. Whether you're a foodie looking for the best local restaurants, an adventure seeker wanting off-the-beaten-path experiences, or a culture enthusiast seeking authentic local interactions, TripWell has you covered.

Adam Cole, Founder

The Team

TripWell is built by a team of travel enthusiasts, AI experts, and product designers who are passionate about making travel planning effortless and enjoyable. We've traveled the world, experienced the frustration of generic travel guides, and are committed to creating something better.

Our mission is to help you discover the world's most amazing places, connect with local cultures, and create memories that last a lifetime. Because the best trips aren't just about the destinations - they're about the experiences, the people, and the stories you'll tell for years to come.
