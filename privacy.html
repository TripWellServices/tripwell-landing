Privacy Policy

How we protect your data

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, plan a trip, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Travel preferences and interests
  • Trip details and itineraries
  • Feedback and reviews

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our AI-powered trip planning services
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your trips and our services
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we regularly review our security practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@tripwell.app
